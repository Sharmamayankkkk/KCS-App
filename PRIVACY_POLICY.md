# Privacy Policy for KCS Meet

**Last Updated:** December 27, 2024  
**Effective Date:** December 27, 2024

## 1. Introduction

Krishna Consciousness Society ("we," "our," or "us") operates KCS Meet, a video conferencing platform designed for spiritual communities. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web services.

**By using KCS Meet, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.**

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information You Provide

We collect the following personal information when you create an account:

- **Name** (First name and Last name)
- **Email Address** (for account creation and authentication)
- **User ID** (automatically generated unique identifier)
- **Profile Picture** (optional, uploaded by you)
- **Username** (for display in meetings)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use KCS Meet, we automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Meeting participation history, feature usage, session duration
- **Log Data**: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and date of visits
- **Location Data**: Approximate location (city/country level) based on IP address
- **Audio and Video Data**: During meetings (processed in real-time, not stored unless recording is enabled)

### 2.3 Payment Information

When you make payments through Super Chat:

- **Transaction Amount and Currency**
- **Payment Method Type** (via Cashfree payment gateway)
- **Transaction ID and Status**
- **Phone Number** (Future Implementation - will be collected in v2.0)

**Note:** We do not store your credit card, debit card, or bank account details. All payment information is processed and secured by Cashfree, our PCI-DSS compliant payment processor.

**About Phone Number Collection (Future):**
In a future update (v2.0), we will request your phone number when you make your first Super Chat payment. This is required by our payment processor (Cashfree) for:
- Payment processing compliance
- Transaction verification and security
- Fraud prevention
- SMS notifications (optional)

Phone number collection will be:
- **Optional**: Only required if you use Super Chat feature
- **Secure**: Encrypted and stored securely
- **Limited Sharing**: Only shared with Cashfree for payment processing
- **Deletable**: Can be deleted upon request (except for completed transaction records retained for 7 years per tax law)
- **Not Currently Implemented**: Developer phone number is currently hardcoded for testing purposes and will be replaced with user phone numbers in future version

### 2.4 Meeting Content

- **Chat Messages**: Text messages sent during meetings
- **Meeting Recordings**: Audio and video recordings (only when recording feature is activated by meeting host)
- **Poll Responses**: Your votes and responses to in-meeting polls
- **Super Chat Messages**: Paid messages and associated metadata

### 3.4 Communications

- **Service Updates**: Notify you about service changes, updates, and maintenance
- **Support**: Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Retention Periods

- **Account Data**: Retained while your account is active and for 90 days after account deletion
- **Meeting Recordings**: Stored as configured by meeting host, default 180 days
- **Chat Messages**: Retained for 90 days after meeting ends
- **Payment Records**: Retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance
- **Log Data**: Retained for 90 days for security and debugging purposes

### 5.2 Deletion Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data. See Section 9 for details.

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

### 6.1 Technical Safeguards

- **Encryption in Transit**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher
- **Encryption at Rest**: Sensitive data stored in databases is encrypted
- **Secure Authentication**: Password hashing using bcrypt, JWT token-based sessions
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access control (RBAC) for administrative functions
- **Regular Security Audits**: Periodic security assessments and vulnerability scanning

### 6.2 Physical Safeguards

- **Cloud Infrastructure**: Hosted on secure, SOC 2 compliant cloud providers
- **Data Centers**: Industry-leading data centers with physical security controls

### 6.3 Administrative Safeguards

- **Employee Training**: Regular security awareness training for staff
- **Limited Access**: Access to personal data restricted to authorized personnel only
- **Incident Response**: Documented procedures for responding to security incidents

**Note:** While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

### 9.1 How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account through:

1. **In-App Method**:
   - Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
   - Confirm deletion request
   - You will receive email confirmation

2. **Email Request**:
   - Send email to divineconnectionkcs@gmail.com
   - Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
   - Include your registered email address
   - We will process within 7 business days

3. **Web Portal**:
   - Visit: https://meet.krishnaconsciousnesssociety.com/account/delete
