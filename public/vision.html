Our Vision & Mission

From Community Tool to Global Platform

KCS Meet was born from a simple need: to connect our community within the Krishna Consciousness Society (KCS). It was built in India, by our community, for our community.

As the platform matured, it evolved into a powerful, modern video conferencing solution with features comparable to global leaders. We recognized its potential to serve a much broader audience.

The Expansion: A New Chapter

We now aim to expand KCS Meet beyond the society, transforming it into a public, revenue-generating platform. Our goal is to empower not only the Krishna Consciousness community but also individuals, organizations, and creators worldwide.

Our Core Mission

Our goal is to build India’s first spiritually inspired communication platform — blending devotion, design, and digital innovation — and to establish a sustainable source of growth and income for the Krishna Consciousness Society.
