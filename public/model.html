A one-page overview of how KCS Meet creates, delivers, and captures value.

  • Partnerships & Affiliate Programs
  • Word-of-mouth within communities

Cost Structure

  • Cloud Infrastructure (Vercel, Stream, Supabase)
  • Payment Processing Fees
  • Employee Salaries (Dev, Marketing, Support)
  • Marketing & Advertising Spend
